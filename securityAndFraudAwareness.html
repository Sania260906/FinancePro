Incident Report 1: Phishing Scam

Date: March 10, 2025

A phishing scam targeted bank customers with fake emails, tricking them into entering login details on fraudulent websites.

Impact: 5,000+ victims, $2M lost.

Prevention: Avoid clicking unknown links, enable MFA, report suspicious emails.

Incident Report 2: Ransomware Attack

Date: February 28, 2025

A hospital network suffered a ransomware attack, encrypting patient records and demanding a $5M ransom.

Impact: 1.2M patient records locked, operations halted for 72 hours.

Prevention: Use strong cybersecurity policies, and back up data regularly.
